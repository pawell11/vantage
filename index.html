Architecture

From raw feed to operational event.

Four stages. Engineered for speed, accuracy, and continuity.

  1. 01 — Ingest

    Ingest

    RTSP, WebRTC, ONVIF, and drone SDKs into a unified buffer with per-feed sync.

  2. 02 — Perceive

    Perceive

    Frame-level detection, tracking, and embedding extraction at the edge.

  3. 03 — Fuse

    Fuse

    Cross-camera handoff and re-identification into persistent subject objects.

  4. 04 — Surface

    Surface

    Ranked events, alerts, and structured evidence — not raw clips.
